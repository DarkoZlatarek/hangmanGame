Hangman Game

Welcome to the Hangman Game!

The game will generate an animal for you to guess.

Chose the letter on the keyboard on the screen to start guessing the animal letter by letter.

If you are stuck, click on the top right corner to help you with the answer.

Be careful, if you guess the letter wrongly six times, you lose. However, not to worry, click on and your game starts again.

If you have too many losses and feel a bit ashamed, you can always click and the scores will go to zero.

Good luck and most importantly, have fun!

*click anywhere on the screen to close this window
